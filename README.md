Noctis is an agentic web platform that helps CS graduates and other technology professionals assess their skills by providing mock interview rounds tailored to their profiles.

## Problem

Many CS graduates are unfamiliar with how real interviews are conducted. Universities provide limited opportunities for students to experience interviews, and not every student gets a chance to participate. As a result, many students have only a limited understanding of the career path they choose.

## Solution

Noctis gathers information from a user's resume and creates a personalized profile based on it. It interacts with users according to their profile, identifies areas for improvement, and provides guidance on how to strengthen their skills. It also generates mock interview rounds and helps users improve their performance throughout the process, all for free.

## Notable Features

### 1. Types of Users

- Frontend Engineer
- Backend Engineer
- Full Stack Engineer (included in MVP)
- AI Engineer
- Data Scientist

### 2. Resume Parsing

- Extracts summary
- Extracts work experience
- Extracts skills
- Extracts projects
- Extracts social links

### 3. Mock Interview Rounds

- Generates MCQs based on the user's skills.
- Generates LeetCode-style coding questions based on the user's years of experience (YOE), with an integrated code editor.
- Generates project walkthrough questions derived from the user's projects, presented in Markdown format.
- Generates system design questions based on the user's years of experience (YOE), supported by Markdown and a design canvas.

### 4. Results & Feedback

- Provides detailed performance analysis and feedback after each interview round.

### 5. Gamification & Experience Points

- Rewards users with experience points (XP) based on their performance and progress.

### 6. Personal Chatbot

- A personalized AI chatbot that provides career guidance, interview preparation assistance, and recommendations tailored to the user's profile.
